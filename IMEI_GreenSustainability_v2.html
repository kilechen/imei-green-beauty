Our Why

Initiative 01

Pineapple Leaf Extract — Farm Waste to Face Care

Taiwan grows over 400,000 tons of pineapple a year. The leaves? Usually burned. We partnered with ITRI (Taiwan's Industrial Technology Research Institute) to extract skincare-grade actives from those leaves. Now they go into cleansers and serums instead of smoke.

A real circular ingredient story your brand can tell.

This isn't a marketing angle — it's a verified, government-backed R&D program. We're actively developing more upcycled botanicals from local agriculture.

Initiative 02

Smart Circular Box — Zero-Waste Shipping

Every shipment between suppliers, our factory, and brand clients used to mean disposable cardboard. Our Smart Circular Box replaces that with a reusable, IoT-tracked container. It gets shipped, received, returned, cleaned, and used again.
