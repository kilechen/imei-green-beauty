Who We Are
A Heritage Manufacturer Built for Today's Market
IMEI Cosmetics (益美化工) is a Taiwan-based beauty manufacturer with over 70 years of formulation and production expertise. We specialize in ODM and OBM services, helping brands bring high-performance products to market — from concept to shelf-ready delivery. Our strength lies in formulations designed for hot, humid, and subtropical climates, where product stability, skin feel, and long-wear performance truly matter.
